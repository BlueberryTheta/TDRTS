What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help operate and improve the website and to provide certain features.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential: to enable core features of the site.
  • Preferences: to remember settings and options.
  • Advertising: to support ad delivery from third‑party vendors (e.g., Google).

Your Choices

  • You can disable or delete cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not function without them.
  • To learn more about Google’s advertising cookies and your choices, visit Google’s Ads Settings.
